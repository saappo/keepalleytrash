Action Needed!

The future of alley collection is uncertain. Immediate community action is required to keep our alleys clean and safe. Get involved now to make a difference!

Your Role in Maintaining Clean Alleys

While we continue discussions with the city, here's what we can do to maintain our alleys and show our commitment to clean, safe neighborhoods.

Essential Maintenance Responsibilities

  • Litter Removal

    Keep areas from the front curb to the alley centerline free of litter to ensure clean public spaces.

  • Obstruction Removal

    Remove obstructions, such as large architectural rocks, from the right-of-way and utility easements.

  • Grass and Weed Control

    Maintain grass and weeds below 12 inches from the front curb to the alley centerline to prevent nuisances and fire hazards.

  • Vegetation Overgrowth

    Trim vegetation to provide at least 15 feet of clearance above the alley, keeping it accessible for vehicles and pedestrians.

Rollcart Best Practices

Proper Rollcart Placement
  • Place rollcarts with proper clearance from obstructions
  • Ensure carts are not overloaded
  • Place recyclables loose in blue carts

Reporting Issues

Report alley surface issues to the City through:

Dallas.gov Dallas 311 Mobile App Call 3-1-1
Important Note

Pouring concrete or filling ruts in the alley is NOT a resident responsibility. These are structural improvements that fall under city maintenance.
