About Keep Alley Trash

Neighbors Building Community Solutions

Neighbors attempting to build something to help keep our valued city services.

Keep Alley Trash is a community-driven platform created by neighbors who understand the importance of maintaining clean and safe neighborhoods. We believe that strong communities start with engaged residents who work together to preserve the services that make our city great.

When the City of Dallas announced plans to end alley trash collection for over 30,000 customers, we knew we had to act. This platform serves as a hub for neighbors to coordinate cleanup efforts, share information, and build stronger connections within our communities.

Our mission is simple: Keep our alleys clean, our neighborhoods safe, and our community connected.

What We Do:
  • Organize alley cleanup events
  • Share neighborhood guidelines and best practices
  • Connect neighbors for community initiatives
  • Provide resources for maintaining clean alleys
  • Build stronger, more engaged communities

Built with care by neighbors, for neighbors
